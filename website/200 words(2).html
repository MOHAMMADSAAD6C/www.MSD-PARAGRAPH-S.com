Trees are very important to us. This is due to plants and trees that we are able to survive on this planet. The tree extends life giving oxygen without which it will not be possible for humans or other species to survive. However, this is not the only reason that trees are important to us. They have a lot to do with the environment as well as living beings. In addition to giving oxygen, trees also absorb various harmful gases from the environment, thereby reducing the impact of global warming. Trees also provide food and shelter to us. Many trees bear fruit, which serve as food for birds and animals. Humans give some names to various fruits such as mangoes, apples and bananas. The leaves of trees, roots and bark are used to prepare medicines. Trees also provide shelter to animals and humans. Large, dense forests serve as wildlife habitats and contribute to rich biodiversity. Wood and other materials derived from trees are used to craft many things which are essential for a comfortable life.

Trees also make the environment calm and calm. They help people meet and socialize. Parks with small green trees and plants are preferred among small children and elderly people. They often gather here to participate in various outdoor activities. We should save trees and plants to keep our environment clean and promote a healthy and happy life.
