Trees play an important role in balancing and maintaining the Earth's ecological system. Green trees and plants fill the atmosphere with oxygen, clean the air, prevent soil erosion, support wildlife and assist in climate control. Here is a brief look at how trees help in maintaining the ecosystem in all these processes. Purification of air Our environment is filled with all kinds of harmful gases including carbon dioxide, carbon monoxide and sulfur dioxide. Increasing number of vehicles on the road and the increasing number of factories around the world is increasing the level of these harmful gases in the environment.
